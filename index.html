About me


Hi, my name is Sibylle and I'm currently a MSc Student in Computer Science and hold a Bachelors in Economics and Management Science where I learned about Statistics, how people make decisions and how to make good decisions with data. I like learning new things, be it a new programming language or framework, how to cook a new tasty meal or how to assemble a new piece of Ikea furniture. I also like travelling to new places where I haven't been, learn about their different cultures and go hiking in their surroundings. I am really interested in how technology can transform learning for young people and break up the more traditional approach of books and blackboards, which is why I interned as a Digital Intern in the Tech Group at Oxford University Press. Recently, I've also been more interested in games and cyber security which I will explore in more detail over summer. Watch this space! When I don't code, you find me watching Sci-Fi films or reading dystopian literature.
