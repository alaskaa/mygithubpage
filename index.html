About me


Hi, my name is Sibylle and I'm currently a MSc Student in Computer Science. I am fairly new to coding and programming. I only started to look at code and learn the basics when I was 22 years old when I was looking for a more interesting elective than the ones that were on offer in my Business School and took an Introduction to Programming course. My undergraduate was in Economics and whilst I still like Economics, I love everything Tech! I am mostly self-taught and try to pick things up as I go along. I am especially interested in how technology can transform learning for young people and break up the more traditional approach of books and blackboards, which is why I interned as a Digital Intern in the Tech Group at Oxford University Press. When I don't code, you find me watching Sci-Fi films or reading dystopian literature.
